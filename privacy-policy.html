Privacy Policy

Piper Audio processes text, files, and generated audio for the purpose of delivering AI speech, narration, and listening workflows through the platform.

Uploaded documents, submitted text, generated scripts, and output audio may be handled temporarily by the server so the experience can provide playback, download links, and workflow continuity.

Technical logs may include timestamps, IP addresses, request metadata, and error details to support security, reliability, and performance monitoring.

Local history features shown in some workflows are stored in the user's browser with localStorage and are not automatically shared across devices.

If you deploy the platform publicly, review your storage, cleanup, retention, and logging policies carefully before processing sensitive content.
