Privacy Policy

This PDF to Audio Converter processes uploaded PDF files for the purpose of extracting text and generating audio output. The service does not require account creation for normal use.

Uploaded files may be temporarily handled by the server to complete conversion and provide playback or download links. If you deploy this project publicly, review your hosting setup and retention policy before processing sensitive documents.

Basic technical logs may be collected for security, error tracking, and performance monitoring. Those logs can include IP addresses, timestamps, and request metadata.

Audio history saved by the interface is stored locally in the user’s browser with localStorage. That browser history is not shared across devices.

For privacy-sensitive deployments, we recommend setting up automatic file cleanup, restricted logging, and a clear data-retention policy in production.
