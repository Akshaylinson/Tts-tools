Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

What is Piper Audio?

Piper Audio is a professional AI audio creation platform that transforms text, documents, articles, and stories into natural-sounding narration. We provide five specialized workflows for speech generation, podcast creation, document listening, and audio review.

Which workflows are available?

The platform includes AI Audio Proofreader (hear your writing), Blog to Podcast Converter (article narration), PDF to Audio Converter (document listening), Text to Audio Converter (speech generation), and AI Story Narration Generator (immersive storytelling).

Who is this platform for?

Piper Audio is designed for content creators, educators, marketers, writers, and professionals who need high-quality AI narration for scripts, articles, documents, podcasts, and accessibility purposes.

Is the audio quality professional?

Yes. We use advanced AI voice technology to generate natural-sounding speech with proper pacing, intonation, and clarity. The output is suitable for professional use without additional editing.

How do I get started?

Choose the workflow that matches your content type: text for speech generation, PDF for document listening, article URL for podcast creation, or draft text for audio proofreading. Each tool guides you through a simple process.
